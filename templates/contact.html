You can reach us using various communication channels.
To reach the community, consider going to the IRC channel or the Mailing List.
For private inquiries, please leave me an email.


IRC

The official IRC channel is #darm on irc.freenode.net.


Mailing List

You can subscribe to the darm mailing list here. Don't expect it to be very crowded or anything, but any questions you may have will be answered.
If you don't want to subscribe, but simply want to post an email to the mailing list, you can send an email to darm@jbremer.org (which is the address of the mailing list.) Do note that these emails will be manually approved, hence it may take more than a minute to get them on to the mailing list.
